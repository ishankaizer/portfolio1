Music tiles are populated from Ishan's public Spotify playlist
(src/data/music.ts), with cover art referenced from Spotify's CDN.

To override a specific cover with a local image, set that track's `cover`
field in src/data/music.ts to /music/<name>.jpg and drop the file here.
To refresh the whole list, re-run the Spotify fetch that generated
src/data/music.ts.
